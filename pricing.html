Common questions

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — 25% is due upfront before work begins. The remaining 75% is due upon delivery of the finished product. Only the initial deposit is refundable if the project is cancelled before work starts.

What happens after delivery?

Every project includes a 7-day free bug support window after delivery. If something breaks or doesn't work as scoped, I'll fix it at no charge. After that, ongoing support is available via a support plan.

Who owns the code?

You do, 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, documentation, and assets are transferred to you with no strings attached.

What if my project is between tiers?

Just reach out. Most projects get a custom quote anyway — the tiers are a starting point, not a rigid menu.

Do you offer retainers?

Yes. If you need ongoing development, maintenance, or new features after launch, I offer monthly retainer arrangements. Ask me about it during your scoping call.

Keep your system healthy.
Stay covered.

Every system I build runs in production. APIs update, integrations shift, new features come up. A support plan keeps your system sharp and keeps me in your corner. Without one, maintenance falls entirely on you.
