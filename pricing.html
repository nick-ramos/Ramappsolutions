FAQ

Common questions

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — we require a 25% deposit upfront before work begins, with the remaining 75% due on delivery. For Enterprise engagements, payment milestones are scoped per project.

Who owns the code?

You do, 100%. Upon final payment, all source code, documentation, and assets are transferred to you with no strings attached.

What if my project is between tiers?

Just reach out. Most projects get a custom quote anyway — the tiers are a starting point, not a rigid menu.

Do you offer retainers?

Yes. If you need ongoing development, maintenance, or new features after launch, we offer monthly retainer arrangements. Ask us about it during your scoping call.
