Most small businesses are sitting on something valuable — and no easy way to share it.

A volleyball coach I work with had years of player feedback trapped inside individual video clips. Great coaching, going nowhere.

So we turned it into a real platform. The same feedback now does two jobs at once:

🏐 A searchable training library for the coaches he's developing — filter by skill, watch the breakdown, learn his method.

🎯 Private feedback for each player — they tap one link (no login, nothing to install) and see only their own clips, mistakes, and fixes.

The tricky part wasn't the AI that drafts each breakdown — it was privacy. A player sees only their feedback. Coaches learn from it as a teaching example but never see whose it is. That's built into the foundation, not bolted on top.
