Common questions

How much does setup actually take?

Typically 5–7 business days from our kickoff call. I configure your pipeline, connect your accounts, build your brand voice profile, and run a test batch before flipping it live. You don't do anything during setup beyond one 45-minute onboarding call.

Can I control what gets posted?

Yes. Every Monday you'll receive a topic list for the week. You approve, remove, or swap any topic. Nothing goes live without your sign-off on the topic direction. You can also set hard rules ("never post about X") and those are baked into the system permanently.

Will the content sound like me?

That's part of onboarding. I build a brand voice profile — tone, language, topics to emphasize, things to avoid — and the AI uses that to write every script. The output sounds like your brand, not like a generic bot.

What if I want to pause for a week?

Easy. Just let me know. The pipeline can be paused and restarted without losing your account standing or analytics history. No penalty, no charge for paused months.

Do I own the content and the system?

You own all the content — every video, caption, and script is yours. On the Agency plan, I also deliver full source code. On Starter and Pro, the system runs on my infrastructure with you as the exclusive client on your pipeline.

Does this work for niche industries?

Absolutely. The trend engine is configurable — I can scope it to your specific industry, topic pillars, and competitor landscape. Niche industries often see better results because the content is more targeted and less saturated.
