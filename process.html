Scope & Strategy

We kick off with a focused call — usually 45–60 minutes — to understand your problem, your data, your team, and what success looks like. No generic intake forms. Real questions, real answers.

You leave with a written spec, a timeline, and a fixed price. No surprises downstream.

  • Problem definition & goal alignment
  • Data and system audit
  • Written scope & delivery timeline
  • Fixed-price proposal
Timeline 1–2 days
02

Build & Iterate

We build in short sprints with frequent check-ins. You see real, working software early — not slides or mockups. Feedback is fast and incorporated quickly.

We use whatever stack makes the most sense for the problem, not whatever is trendy. Maintainability matters.

  • Sprint-based development (1–2 week cycles)
  • Working demos at each milestone
  • Async updates + sync calls as needed
  • Code reviews & quality checks throughout
Timeline 1–4 weeks
03

Deploy & Hand Off

We handle deployment — whether that's a cloud server, a scheduled job, a containerized service, or an embedded script. Your system goes live and stays live.

You get full documentation, clean code, and a walkthrough so your team can operate it confidently from day one.

  • Production deployment & configuration
  • Full documentation & runbooks
  • Walkthrough session with your team
  • Post-launch support window (14–60 days)
Timeline 1–3 days
